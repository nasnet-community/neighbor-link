Setup Outline Relay
Outline Relay: This section assists you in converting Outline keys. By converting the Outline key, the client will connect through the Iran internet and your Starlink to the Outline server. You can use either a free Outline key or your own Outline key for this process.
Instruction:
  • Copy the Outline key and paste it into the designated converting area.
  • Click the "Convert" button.
  • Use the converted configuration displayed in the blue box within the Outline client.
  • If you are using your own Outline manager, you can configure the new host and port for all of your keys.
